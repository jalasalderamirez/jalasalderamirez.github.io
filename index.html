José A. Lasalde-Ramírez

About

José A. Lasalde-Ramírez is a first-year PhD student at the Andrew and Peggy Cherng Department of Medical Engineering at Caltech. As recipient of the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship (NSF-GRF), his current research at Wei Gao’s group focuses on the fabrication of minimally invasive wearable technologies to monitor and diagnose different diseases. Outside of research, he enjoys watching/playing sports and obsessing over Puerto Rican food. During his graduate career, José hopes to promote Caltech’s commitment to a diverse environment and motivate more students from Puerto Rico and other underrepresented communities to join the institution.
